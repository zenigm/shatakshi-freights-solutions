About Shatakshi Freight Solutions

Shatakshi Freight Solutions started in 2010 as Allahabad Transport Organisation to provide domestic full and part‑load logistics services across India. In 2018 it was incorporated as Shatakshi Freight Solutions with branches in major cities nationwide.

The company and its fleet of trucks have crossed many milestones in delivering safe, on‑time road transport and is geared to meet every expectation through customer satisfaction and delight.

✓ Professional & Experienced

Over a decade of experience handling industrial, commercial and project cargo with dedicated drivers and operational staff.

✓ Customer‑First Approach

✓ Pan‑India Presence

Major branches at Ahmedabad, Bangalore, Bhopal, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Raipur, Vapi and more.
